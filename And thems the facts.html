The best conclusion to a passage

"and thems the facts"

Whilst idling amidst the expanse of Youtube, my eyes chanced upon a discourse concerning the most curious phrase, "and thems the facts" as an alternative to the trite "in conclusion".

The commanding implications of unwavering conviction and authoritative preeminence this phrase conveyed caused a profound sense of astonishment to permeate my being.

And so, embracing this novel colloquialism, I began to endow my speech and writing with an invigorating sense of potency, tempered with prudence to guard against overuse.

In conclusion? Nay, I shan't resort to such hackneyed verbiage. I leave you with this indelible maxim: And thems the facts, fellow humans.
